White areas

Aerolineas Argentinas

White areas around, and between the elements of this design are carefully considered to improve focus and comprehension. While the user prepares for a trip, he will value the space between elements indicating distinction. This clearly helps the user in order to feel confident while booking.

Balance

The elements of this design are symmetrically and equally balanced, this creates a sacred and formal environment according to the religious content of the music played by the choir.
